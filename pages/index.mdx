## Why teams pick Streams

- **Two identifier spaces, one consistent key.** Our internal Universal
  Instrument Record (UIR) is stable across the data layer; the provider's
  instrument token is opaque to your code. We resolve the join for you.
- **No silent price drift.** Every tick is keyed to the same UIR your search
  result returned. The price you saw in search is the price you'll see in
  the live tick.
- **Resilient by default.** Backpressure, reconnects, and stale-tick
  detection are built in. You focus on the UI; we focus on the wire.
- **Daily provider session refresh is automatic.** The platform handles the
  underlying market data provider's daily access token refresh; you keep a
  single long-lived API key.
